What's needed to run it
Developer Ongoing
Keeps the live app stable and compliant — bug fixes, App Store & Play Store updates, performance, and rolling out new features as we grow.
Social Media Manager Instagram + TikTok
Runs both platforms end to end — daily posting, community management, replies, trend-jacking and tracking what's working across the widest reach.
Video Editor Full
Turns every shoot, activation and idea into a steady daily stream of Reels, TikToks and ad cuts — a full editing schedule, nothing left on the table.
Content Designer Full
Carousels, static posts, ad creative and brand assets — a steady flow of on-brand design feeding both platforms every week.
Social Media Manager Instagram only
Runs Instagram end to end — daily posting, community management, replies and performance tracking. TikTok comes in with Option A.
Video Editor Part-time
A lighter run of short-form edits each week — enough to keep the channel active and posting, with fewer cuts overall.
Content Designer Part-time
The essentials — key posts and ad creative to keep the feed looking sharp, at a lighter volume.
Same core team. The difference is reach and output.

Option A runs both Instagram and TikTok with full video and design output — the most ground covered and the best shot at traction. Option B keeps it lean and Instagram-focused, with lighter video and design to protect runway. My honest steer is Option A: the production you're already funding — shoots, models, activations — only pays off if the team has the capacity to turn it into content. Either way, you get a real team and a clean number you can plan around.
